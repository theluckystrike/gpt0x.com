About GPT0X

Advanced language model engineering for practitioners who build with LLMs.

Our Mission

GPT0X exists because the gap between using an LLM and engineering with LLMs is vast and growing. Anyone can send a prompt to an API, but building production systems that are reliable, cost-effective, and measurably good requires deep technical knowledge spanning model selection, prompt engineering, fine-tuning, evaluation, and deployment optimization. We provide the technical depth that serious practitioners need.

Our content focuses on the engineering challenges that matter in practice: how to fine-tune models that actually improve on the base model for your specific task, how to build RAG systems that retrieve the right information and generate faithful answers, how to evaluate LLM outputs systematically when "correct" is subjective, and how to serve models at scale without burning through your compute budget. Every guide is based on real implementation experience and includes the practical details that tutorials often skip.

We cover both the commercial LLM ecosystem (GPT-4, Claude, Gemini) and the rapidly maturing open-source landscape (LLaMA, Mistral, Qwen, Phi). Understanding both sides is essential because the right choice depends on your constraints, and those constraints change as models improve and costs decrease. We help you navigate these tradeoffs with data-driven analysis rather than hype.

GPT0X is part of a network of specialized AI and ML resources. Our flagship resource, zovo.one, provides a comprehensive directory of AI tools spanning the entire ecosystem. Together with our partner sites, we aim to cover every aspect of modern AI development.
